B: Filled Geometric

Solid filled letter-forms, like architectural A's without crossbars. The overlap zone creates a denser visual where the inner strokes merge. Bolder, heavier presence. Reads well as a brand stamp.

C: Diamond Intersection

Stroke-based like A, but the overlap zone is highlighted with a subtle diamond outline where the inner legs cross. Adds a focal point and a sense of depth. The diamond could pulse or glow in animated contexts.
